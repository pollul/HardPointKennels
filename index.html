Welcome to HARD POINT KENNELS. We have relocated from Southern California to  Mann Creek Valley area of Weiser, Idaho. We are within easy driving distance of Hells Canyon as well as many other popular upland bird hunting destinations. There are also many upland bird hunting clubs in the region for those that prefer a more controlled hunting environment.


We specialize in quality bred German Shorthaired Pointer gun dogs. We strive to provide dogs that are loaded with natural drive, desire to please, style/class, and will not only hunt hard but be pleasant with the family as well. Our bloodlines include several proven field champions and provide dogs with natural ability in all phases of gun dog performance


Hard Point Kennels is a premier training and breeding facility. We have been training hunting dogs for more than 30 years. We limit the number of client dogs that we take in at one time to make sure the dogs get proper attention during their stay with us. Additionally we only breed a minimum number of puppies each year so that we can maintain the highest quality prospects. Our current line of dogs is a proven group of select dogs that we have taken great pride in selecting to provide a foundation for continued success here at our kennel.


Please feel free to contact us if you any questions. We appreciate you visiting our site.
